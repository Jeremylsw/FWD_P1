ITINERARY

DAY 1

Morning

Take your selected flight from Singapore to Tokyo Narita Airport

Afternoon

Embark on a water sightseeing bus tour running between the Sumida River and Odaiba with a total of 8 stops along the way. This Mirai sightseeing ship was specially designed by Japanese animation master Reiji Matsumoto. It has a cool appearance and looks like a teardrop and the ehtire hull is covered with a glass roof. Enjoy the breathtaking scenery of Tokyo on both sides of the strait while sitting in this stunning ship.

Evening

End the night at Takanawa Hanakohro hotel.

DAY 2

Morning - Afternoon

Spend the day at Tokyo DisneySea and Tokyo Disneyland. First opened in 2001, the entire park sports a nautical theme and has seven main areas or “ports of call.” Each area features a number of rides, attractions, and entertainment venues based on characters, stories, and movies from the Disney collection. With lots to do and a true plethora of entertainment options, you will find yourself wanting to return again and again.

Evening

End your night at Tokyo's 3-star accomodation - Tune Inn Ebisu #2 located conveniently in the center of Tokyo.

DAY 3

Morning

Visit the Meiji Jingu - a Shinto shrine dedicated to the Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken. It is located in Tokyo’s Shibuya ward. The shrine is surrounded by a forest park consisting of over 120,000 trees donated by people from all parts of Japan. Together with the adjacent Yoyogi Park, Meiji Jingu is part of a large greenspace in central Tokyo. Enormous torii, or gates, mark the entrance to the shrine complex. Because of its size and the relative seclusion it offers, Meiji Jingu is an extremely popular location.

Afternoon

Head over to Tokyo Skytree, the world's tallest tower at 634 meters and enjoy Tokyo's paranomic view from the top of Sumida City.

Evening

After your Tokyo Skytree experience, end your day off at Aman Tokyo located nearby the skytree for a good nights rest.

DAY 4

Morning - Afternoon

Head over to Sanrio Puroland, also known as Hello Kitty Land, Tokyo's indoor amusement park based on Sanrio cartoon stars Hello Kitty, My Melody, Pom Pom Purin, and Jewelpet. Fantasy-like, inviting, and full of creativity, it is a popular attraction for both children and adults.

Evening

Check into Keio Plaza Hotel Tama near Sanrio Puroland where you'll be staying for the night.

DAY 5

Morning

TeamLab Planets is a museum where you walk through water, and a garden where you become one with the flowers. There are four massive exhibition spaces and two gardens. By immersing the entire body with other people in these massive body immersive artworks, the boundary between the body and the artwork dissolves, the boundaries between the self, others, and the world become continuous, and we explore a new relationship without boundaries between ourselves and the world.

Afternoon

Located in a western portion of Tokyo, the Ghibli Museum is dedicated to showcasing the work of the Japanese animation house Studio Ghibli. Designed by Studio Ghibli director Hayao Miyazaki, the museum is heavily influenced by European architectural styles. Miyazaki used film storyboards to sketch out a plan inspired by locations such as the hilltop village of Calcata in Italy. Full of everything related to Ghibli animations, visitors can not only take a look at the animation buildings, they can also see original Ghibli works and other animation materials. The museum’s souvenir shop and themed restaurant are also very popular.

Evening

Your trip has been completed. Have a safe flight back to Singapore from Tokyo. We hope you had an enjoyable time here in Tokyo and we hope to see you again!
